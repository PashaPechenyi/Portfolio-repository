Lauren Hom, Designer and Letterer

2) “Design is a response to a specific problem. You are given a problem to solve, and then you let the problem itself tell you what your solution is.”

Chip Kidd, Book Cover Designer

Michael Bierut, Design Critic, Lecturer, Co-founder of Design Observer

Morag Myerscough, Designer and Artist

Shawna X, Independent Artist and Creative
